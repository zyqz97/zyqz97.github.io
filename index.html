Yuqi Zhang   张煜奇

I am currently a Ph.D student (from Sep. 2022) at the Future Network of Intelligence Institute (FNii) and the School of Science and Engineering (SSE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen), under the supervision of Prof. Guanying Chen and Prof. Shuguang Cui.

I received B.Eng. and M.S. degrees from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) in 2019 and 2022.

My research interests are in computer vision. Specifically, learning-based methods for 3D vision, neural rendering, etc.
